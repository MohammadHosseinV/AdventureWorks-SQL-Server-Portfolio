# Sales Analysis

## Business Problem

Management requires visibility into revenue trends and sales performance over time.

## Objective

Analyze historical sales data to identify trends, growth patterns, and average order values.

## Tables Used

* Sales.SalesOrderHeader

## SQL Concepts

* Aggregate Functions
* GROUP BY
* ORDER BY
* Date Functions

## Reports Included

### Yearly Revenue

Total revenue generated per year.

### Monthly Revenue

Revenue trend by month.

### Average Order Value

Average amount spent per order.
